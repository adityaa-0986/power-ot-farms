Our Harvest

Fresh from Our Waters

Premium Tilapia

Renowned for its mild, sweet flavor and firm texture. Our Tilapia is raised in nutrient-rich waters, ensuring a high-quality product perfect for a variety of culinary delights.

Hearty Catfish

A beloved staple, our Catfish are known for their distinctive, savory taste. Farmed with meticulous care, they provide an excellent source of protein and are central to many traditional dishes.

Future: Fish Feed

We are expanding into fish feed production to ensure a fully sustainable cycle, providing optimal nutrition for our fish and offering quality feed to other farms in the region.

Whole Snapper

Expertly seasoned and pan-seared to perfection. Our whole fish is paired with a vibrant medley of cherry tomatoes and herbs, finished in a creamy Mediterranean-style sauce that elevates every bite.

Grilled Tilapia

Charcoal-grilled to lock in smoky flavor, our tilapia is served with crisp bell peppers and onions for a burst of color and crunch. Accompanied by traditional sauces and a hearty portion of fufu, this dish celebrates the rich culinary heritage of West Africa with every bite.

Spiced Fish

Delicately grilled and seasoned with bold spices, our fish rests atop a refreshing layer of cucumber slices. Paired with golden fries and garnished with vibrant vegetables, this dish blends comfort and flair in every satisfying bite.
