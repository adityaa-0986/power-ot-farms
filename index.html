Our Story

Complete Solutions for Modern Aquaculture

Power OT Farms was founded to provide high-quality fish and farm products while promoting sustainable aquaculture practices in Ghana and West Africa. Whether you are a professional fish farmer or a home consumer, we supply everything you need to raise healthy fish and enjoy nutritious seafood.

From fresh and smoked fish to nutritious feed, fingerlings, and tarpaulin tanks, our goal is to empower farmers, improve food security, and create economic opportunities through innovative fish farming techniques.
